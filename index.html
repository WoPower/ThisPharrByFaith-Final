About TPBF

A Transitional Recovery Home
for Women

This Pharr By Faith is a safe, substance-free residence offering structure, dignity, and spiritual grounding to women rebuilding their lives.

What We Provide

TPBF provides...

Safe & Substance-Free

A stable environment designed to support healing and steady progress.

Trauma-Informed Care

Compassionate, evidence-based support meeting every resident where she is.

Mental Health Support

Recovery-focused services that nurture mind, body, and spirit.

Personalized Case Management

Individual guidance, goal-setting, and resource navigation.

Structure & Accountability

Daily rhythm balanced with empathy, respect, and dignity.

Faith-Based Community

Optional spiritual support rooted in love and belonging.

Who We Serve

Women & U.S. Veterans in recovery seeking stability, support, and growth.

  • Substance use recovery
  • Mental health support
  • Structure and stability
  • Healing and personal growth
Our Approach

Whole-person care, rooted in faith.

  • Faith-based with optional spiritual support
  • Respectful, nonjudgmental, and confidential
  • Whole-person care: mind, body, and spirit
  • Community and peer support

This Pharr By Faith

VISION • MISSION • FOCUS

Vision

Restoring faith, rebuilding lives, and reviving purpose.

Mission

Providing quality residential, aftercare, support services, and training to women recovering from substance use and re-entry challenges.

Focus

Empowering women through support, faith teachings, and life-skills training to achieve long-term recovery and self-sufficiency.

Meet The Director

Leadership shaped by lived experience.

At the heart of This Pharr By Faith is a Veteran and leader who deeply understands the journey. Shaped by lived experience, she leads with faith, empathy, and resilience—offering more than guidance, but clarity, connection, and direction.

Marsha P.'s approach is intentional: meeting people where they are, walking alongside them, and helping them rise with confidence and purpose.
